Heritage & Craftsmanship

The Aura Legacy

A Century of Precision

Founded in 1920 by master horologist Armand Laurent, Aura Timepieces began as an uncompromising pursuit of perfection. In a small workshop nestled in the Swiss Alps, Armand sought to create a watch that would not simply measure time, but capture its eternal essence.

Today, over a century later, we hold true to that founding vision. Each Aura watch remains a symphony of intricate mechanics, luxurious materials, and meticulous hand-assembly, designed for individuals who understand that true value lies in the details.

Our Mission

We exist to bridge the gap between historic craftsmanship and modern design. In a world defined by mass production, we offer rarity and soul. Our mission is to forge heirlooms that transcend generations—timepieces that tell a story before they even tell the time.

Through sustainable sourcing of precious metals and a commitment to preserving traditional watchmaking arts, we define the future of sustainable luxury.

Our Milestone Journey

1920

The Founding

Armand Laurent opens the first Aura workshop in Geneva, releasing the inaugural 'Series One'.

1955

The Automatic Revolution

Aura introduces its first proprietary automatic movement, setting new standards for power reserve.

1998

The Chronograph Elite

Launch of our flagship titanium collection, worn by explorers and pioneers.

2026

Sustainable Luxury

Transitioning to 100% recycled precious metals without compromising a singular facet of quality.
